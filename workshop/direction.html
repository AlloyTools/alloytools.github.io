Directions to Star Conference Room, 32-D463
The Star Conference room is on the 4th floor of the Dreyfoos Wing in the Stata Center (Building 32).

Enter the Dreyfoos Tower (on Vasssar St.) and proceed straight ahead; there will be elevators to the right.
Take the elevators to the 4th floor; exit to the left and then turn right at the end of the elevator bank.
At the end of the short corridor, turn right, just before the R&D Dining room.
The Star Conference Room is straight ahead, just past a set of stairs.
